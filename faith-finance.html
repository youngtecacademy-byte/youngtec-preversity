Faith, Finance and Business

Integrating spiritual values with financial wisdom for lasting success.

Course Overview

This course explores the powerful connection between faith, finance, and business practices. It provides practical insights on how to integrate biblical principles into financial decisions, entrepreneurship, and leadership while building wealth with purpose and integrity.

Objectives

  • Understand the role of faith in financial and business success.
  • Learn ethical financial practices rooted in biblical values.
  • Develop resilience in managing business challenges through faith.
  • Apply spiritual wisdom to create sustainable financial growth.

Outline in Modules

  • Module 1: Biblical Foundations of Finance
  • Module 2: Faith-Driven Business Strategies
  • Module 3: Ethical Wealth Creation
  • Module 4: Overcoming Financial Challenges with Faith
  • Module 5: Building Kingdom-Oriented Businesses

Duration

8 Weeks (Part-Time / Online and Onsite Options)

Who Can Apply

Entrepreneurs, business leaders, faith-based organizations, professionals, and individuals seeking to align their financial journey with spiritual principles.

What You'll Gain

  • Deeper understanding of integrating faith and finance.
  • Skills for ethical and sustainable wealth creation.
  • Confidence in applying spiritual principles in business.
  • Network of like-minded individuals and faith-driven entrepreneurs.

Career Opportunities

  • Faith-Based Business Consultant
  • Ethical Entrepreneur
  • Financial Stewardship Coach
  • Corporate Social Responsibility Specialist
  • Wealth and Business Advisor
