Course Overview

The School of AI equips students with deep knowledge of artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, and automation. From building predictive models to creating AI-driven applications, our program prepares you for the most in-demand careers in tech.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of AI and machine learning algorithms.
  • Develop skills in Python for AI, data preprocessing, and model training.
  • Apply AI in real-world scenarios such as computer vision and NLP.
  • Explore ethical AI practices and responsible technology development.
  • Work on capstone AI projects with industry relevance.

Curriculum Highlights

Semester 1
  • Introduction to AI & Data Science
  • Python Programming for AI
  • Data Wrangling & Visualization
  • Machine Learning Basics
Semester 2
  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision & Image Processing
  • Capstone AI Application Project
