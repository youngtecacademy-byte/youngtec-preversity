Deep Learning & Neural Networks

Explore advanced concepts in AI with deep learning and neural network design.

Course Overview

This course introduces learners to deep learning principles, exploring how artificial neural networks work, how they are trained, and how they can be applied to solve complex real-world problems. You will learn about supervised and unsupervised deep learning, convolutional networks, recurrent networks, and modern AI frameworks.

Objectives

  • Understand the principles of deep learning and neural networks.
  • Gain practical knowledge of training and optimizing deep learning models.
  • Explore architectures like CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers.
  • Apply neural networks to fields like image recognition, NLP, and forecasting.

Course Outline

  • Module 1: Introduction to Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Module 2: Perceptrons, Activation Functions, and Backpropagation
  • Module 3: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  • Module 4: Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) and LSTMs
  • Module 5: Transformers and Modern Architectures
  • Module 6: Applications in Vision, NLP, and Business
  • Module 7: Model Deployment and Real-World Projects

Duration

10 Weeks (Weekend & Evening Classes Available)

Who Can Apply

This course is designed for aspiring AI professionals, data scientists, software engineers, and researchers who want to gain advanced skills in deep learning.

What You'll Gain

  • Hands-on experience with TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Keras.
  • Ability to design, train, and evaluate deep learning models.
  • Practical application of deep learning in multiple industries.
  • Certification in Deep Learning & Neural Networks.

Career Opportunities

  • Deep Learning Engineer
  • Computer Vision Engineer
  • NLP Engineer
  • AI Research Scientist
  • Data Scientist (AI specialization)
