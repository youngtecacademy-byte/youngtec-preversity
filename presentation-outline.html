Presentation Software

Learn to create powerful, engaging, and professional presentations with confidence.

Course Overview

This course equips learners with essential skills in using presentation software (such as Microsoft PowerPoint and Google Slides) to create impactful visual presentations for academic, business, and personal purposes.

Objectives

  • Understand the principles of effective presentations.
  • Learn to design slides with professional layouts and multimedia.
  • Develop storytelling skills using visual aids.
  • Gain confidence in delivering engaging presentations.

Course Outline (Modules)

  • Introduction to Presentation Tools
  • Designing Effective Slides
  • Using Multimedia (Images, Audio, Video)
  • Animation and Transitions
  • Collaborating and Sharing Presentations
  • Final Project: Delivering a Professional Presentation

Duration

4 Weeks (Weekend and Weekday sessions available)

Who Can Apply?

Students, business professionals, educators, and anyone interested in improving presentation skills.

What You'll Gain

  • Ability to design professional slideshows
  • Confidence in public speaking with visual support
  • Creative storytelling through visuals
  • Practical presentation project

Career Opportunities

  • Business Analyst
  • Teacher/Trainer
  • Marketing and Sales Professional
  • Corporate Communicator
