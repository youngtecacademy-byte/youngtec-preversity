Course Overview

Our School of Technology offers cutting-edge training in information systems, network administration, cloud computing, and emerging technologies. Students gain both technical expertise and strategic insight to thrive in the evolving tech landscape.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand modern IT infrastructures and systems management.
  • Gain hands-on skills in network setup, security, and optimization.
  • Explore cloud platforms, virtualization, and DevOps practices.
  • Apply problem-solving techniques to real-world IT challenges.
  • Prepare for global certifications like CompTIA, Cisco, and AWS.

Curriculum Highlights

Semester 1
  • IT Fundamentals & Computer Hardware
  • Networking Essentials
  • Operating Systems & Virtualization
  • Introduction to Cybersecurity
Semester 2
  • Advanced Networking & Security
  • Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Systems Automation & Scripting
  • Capstone IT Infrastructure Project
