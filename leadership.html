Leadership & Ethical Decision-Making

Develop values-driven leadership skills rooted in integrity and faith.

Course Overview

The Leadership & Ethical Decision-Making course equips students with the knowledge, character, and practical tools to lead effectively in diverse environments. It emphasizes integrity, accountability, and faith-based values as guiding principles for leadership in business, ministry, and public life.

Objectives

  • Understand core principles of ethical leadership.
  • Develop decision-making frameworks based on integrity and faith.
  • Learn how to manage people and resources responsibly.
  • Foster accountability and servant-leadership qualities.

Course Outline

  • Module 1: Foundations of Leadership
  • Module 2: Ethical Theories and Applications
  • Module 3: Decision-Making in Crisis
  • Module 4: Faith, Values & Leadership
  • Module 5: Servant Leadership and Accountability
  • Module 6: Leadership Challenges in Modern Society

Duration

12 Weeks (3 Months) – Flexible weekend and weekday sessions available.

Who Can Apply

  • Young professionals and aspiring leaders
  • Business owners and managers
  • Faith and community leaders
  • Students interested in leadership roles

What You'll Gain

  • Strong ethical decision-making skills
  • Improved leadership and management capacity
  • Faith-integrated leadership principles
  • Confidence to lead teams, organizations, and communities

Career Opportunities

  • Business Leadership Roles
  • Faith-based and Nonprofit Leadership
  • Community Development Manager
  • Policy and Ethics Consultant
  • Educational and Institutional Leadership
