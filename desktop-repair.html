Desktop Repair

Master the art of diagnosing and fixing desktop computers

Overview

The Desktop Repair course equips students with hands-on skills to troubleshoot, repair, and maintain desktop computers. You’ll learn about hardware components, operating systems, software installation, and real-world repair techniques to handle common issues.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Desktop Computers
  • Motherboards, Processors & RAM
  • Power Supply and Cooling Systems
  • Installing and Configuring Operating Systems
  • Diagnosing Boot and Performance Issues
  • Troubleshooting Hardware & Software Failures
  • Preventive Maintenance and Best Practices
