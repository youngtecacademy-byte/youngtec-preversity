Laptop & Desktop Procurement Skills

Master the skills of identifying, sourcing, and procuring quality computer systems.

Overview

This course is designed to provide practical skills in procurement of laptops and desktops. Students will learn how to evaluate product quality, negotiate with vendors, understand warranty policies, and ensure cost-effective purchasing. This course is vital for IT managers, entrepreneurs, and technicians aiming to maximize value when sourcing computer systems.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Procurement Fundamentals
  • Understanding Computer Specifications
  • Market Research & Vendor Selection
  • Negotiation & Cost Analysis
  • Warranty & After-Sales Service
  • Bulk Procurement & Logistics
  • Case Studies in IT Procurement
