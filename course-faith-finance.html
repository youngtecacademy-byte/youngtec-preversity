Course Overview

The School of Faith & Finance blends timeless principles of faith with modern strategies in financial management and entrepreneurship. Students are equipped to make informed financial decisions while building a values-driven life and business approach.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the biblical principles of stewardship and wealth creation.
  • Develop essential skills in personal finance and investment.
  • Learn business planning and entrepreneurship strategies.
  • Integrate ethical and faith-based decision-making in finance.
  • Engage in real-life business projects and mentorship programs.

Curriculum Highlights

Semester 1
  • Foundations of Faith & Finance
  • Personal Financial Management
  • Budgeting & Saving Strategies
  • Entrepreneurship Fundamentals
Semester 2
  • Investment & Wealth Building
  • Business Ethics & Leadership
  • Advanced Entrepreneurship
  • Capstone Project: Faith-Driven Business Plan
