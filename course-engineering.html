Course Overview

The School of Engineering at Youngtec Computers Academy provides students with in-depth knowledge of engineering principles, technical skills, and innovative problem-solving approaches. Our programs cover mechanical, electrical, civil, and computer engineering fundamentals, preparing students for impactful careers in diverse industries.

Learning Objectives

  • Master core engineering mathematics and physics concepts.
  • Gain hands-on experience with CAD, simulation, and prototyping tools.
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Work on real-world projects and collaborate in teams.
  • Prepare for professional engineering certifications.

Curriculum Highlights

Semester 1
  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Introduction to Mechanical & Electrical Engineering
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD)
  • Engineering Physics
Semester 2
  • Digital Systems & Microcontrollers
  • Materials Science & Manufacturing
  • Engineering Project Management
  • Capstone Design Project
