Natural Language Processing

Master the art of teaching machines to understand and process human language.

Course Overview

This course introduces the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP), enabling students to explore how machines interpret, generate, and analyze human language. You will learn about text preprocessing, sentiment analysis, speech recognition, chatbots, and large language models.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the foundations of NLP and its applications.
  • Learn techniques for text preprocessing and feature extraction.
  • Explore deep learning methods for NLP such as RNNs and Transformers.
  • Build real-world NLP applications like sentiment analyzers and chatbots.

Course Outline (Modules)

  • Module 1: Introduction to Natural Language Processing
  • Module 2: Text Preprocessing and Feature Engineering
  • Module 3: Language Modeling and Sequence Processing
  • Module 4: Deep Learning for NLP (RNN, LSTM, Transformers)
  • Module 5: Applications of NLP (Chatbots, Sentiment Analysis, Translation)
  • Module 6: Large Language Models and Emerging Trends

Duration

10 Weeks (3 sessions per week, 2 hours per session)

Who Can Apply

Beginners with basic knowledge of Python, data science students, AI enthusiasts, educators, and professionals looking to expand into NLP applications.

What You'll Gain

  • Practical skills in building NLP applications.
  • Understanding of modern NLP architectures like Transformers.
  • Ability to apply NLP in industries such as healthcare, business, and education.
  • A professional certificate in NLP from the School of AI.

Career Opportunities

  • NLP Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • AI Researcher
  • Conversational AI Developer
  • Machine Learning Engineer (specialized in NLP)
