Overview

The Hardware Engineering course equips students with essential skills in computer hardware design, troubleshooting, and system integration. Whether you want to become a PC technician, system engineer, or IT hardware consultant, this course sets the foundation.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Computer Hardware
  • Motherboards, Processors, and Memory
  • Storage Devices and Power Systems
  • Peripheral Devices and Interfaces
  • Diagnostics and Troubleshooting
  • Hands-on Practical Sessions
