Graphic Design & Branding

Master the art of visual communication, creative design, and brand identity development.

Course Overview

This program equips you with both technical expertise and creative strategy to produce compelling designs for print, digital media, and brand marketing. Using industry-standard tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and CorelDRAW, you will gain the skills to design professionally, build brand identities, and present an impressive portfolio for the creative industry.

Detailed Course Outline

  • Module 1: Foundations of Design – Principles of design, color theory, composition, and typography basics. (1 week)
  • Module 2: Adobe Photoshop Essentials – Photo editing, image manipulation, layouts, and digital graphics. (2 weeks)
  • Module 3: Illustrator for Vector Graphics – Logo design, illustration, and scalable vector artworks. (2 weeks)
  • Module 4: CorelDRAW Suite – Practical print design, business cards, flyers, posters, and advanced vector layouts. (2 weeks)
  • Module 5: Branding & Visual Identity – Brand strategy, designing logos, stationery, and cohesive identity systems. (2 weeks)
  • Module 6: Design for Web & Social Media – Creating banners, ads, and visual assets for digital platforms. (1 week)
  • Module 7: Print & Publication Design – Using Adobe InDesign for posters, brochures, magazines, and books. (2 weeks)
  • Module 8: Portfolio Development – Compiling a professional portfolio, client presentation, and freelancing skills. (1 week)

Total Duration: 13 weeks (3 months)
