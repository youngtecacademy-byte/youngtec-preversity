Principles of Financial Stewardship

Discover how to manage resources faithfully and wisely.

Course Overview

This course introduces participants to the biblical and practical principles of financial stewardship. It is designed to help learners develop the skills, discipline, and mindset required to handle money, wealth, and resources in ways that honor God and lead to long-term success.

Objectives

  • Understand the biblical foundation of stewardship.
  • Learn effective money management strategies.
  • Develop habits of accountability and transparency.
  • Balance faith and financial discipline.

Outline (Modules)

  1. Introduction to Stewardship
  2. Biblical Principles of Wealth
  3. Budgeting & Saving Discipline
  4. Debt Management & Giving
  5. Faith, Finance, and Integrity
  6. Creating a Financial Legacy

Duration

6 Weeks (Flexible study options available)

Who Can Apply

This program is open to students, professionals, business leaders, and anyone who wants to master financial stewardship principles grounded in faith.

What You'll Gain

  • Practical tools for managing money wisely
  • Faith-driven approach to financial decisions
  • Confidence in building sustainable wealth
  • Ability to avoid financial pitfalls

Career Opportunities

  • Faith-based Financial Advisor
  • Entrepreneur / Business Owner
  • Stewardship Coach or Consultant
  • Non-profit and Ministry Finance Leader
