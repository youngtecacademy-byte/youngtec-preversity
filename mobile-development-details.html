Mobile Development

Build powerful apps for Android and iOS platforms with modern tools and frameworks.

Overview

Mobile devices are at the center of today’s digital world. This program introduces you to creating applications for Android and iOS platforms using modern development environments like Flutter, React Native, and Kotlin. You’ll gain practical skills to build real-world apps that solve everyday problems.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding mobile ecosystems (Android & iOS).
  • Programming with Flutter, React Native, or native SDKs.
  • User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design for apps.
  • API integration and mobile data storage.
  • Publishing apps to Google Play and Apple App Store.

Duration

12 Weeks (Weekend or Weekday Classes)

Who Can Apply

Beginners in programming, aspiring mobile developers, and anyone interested in creating their own apps for personal or business use.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Mobile App Developers, Full Stack Mobile Engineers, or Freelancers in the app development industry.
