Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are important to us at Youngtec Computers.

1. Introduction

At Youngtec Computers, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details you provide during registration (name, email, phone number).
  • Payment and billing information for course enrollment.
  • Usage data such as visited pages and interactions on our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and manage educational services.
  • Process payments securely.
  • Improve user experience on our platform.
  • Send updates, resources, and important notifications.

4. Data Protection

We implement strong security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or necessary for service provision (e.g., payment processors).

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request clarification on data handling practices.
