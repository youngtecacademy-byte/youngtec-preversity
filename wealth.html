Personal Wealth Management

Master financial planning, investment, and long-term prosperity.

Course Overview

This course provides learners with a comprehensive understanding of how to manage personal finances, build wealth, and achieve financial independence. Participants will learn practical strategies for saving, investing, and protecting their wealth, while aligning financial goals with faith and values.

Objectives

  • Develop financial literacy and money management skills.
  • Learn strategies for long-term investment and savings.
  • Understand risk management and wealth protection.
  • Build a roadmap toward financial independence.

Outline (Modules)

  1. Introduction to Personal Wealth
  2. Budgeting and Expense Management
  3. Investment Principles and Options
  4. Risk Management & Insurance
  5. Retirement and Legacy Planning
  6. Aligning Faith and Financial Goals

Duration

10 Weeks (Blended Learning: Online + Onsite)

Who Can Apply

Individuals seeking to improve personal finances, professionals planning for the future, and anyone committed to building lasting wealth with integrity.

What You'll Gain

  • Confidence in handling personal finances.
  • Tools for effective wealth-building and investments.
  • Ability to protect wealth through smart financial planning.
  • A lifestyle of discipline and financial freedom.

Career Opportunities

  • Personal Finance Consultant
  • Investment Advisor
  • Wealth & Asset Manager
  • Financial Literacy Coach
