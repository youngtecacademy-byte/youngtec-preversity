Graphics Design

Master the art of visual communication and design creativity.

Course Overview

This Graphics Design program equips students with practical skills in digital and print design. Students learn to use tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and CorelDRAW to create stunning visuals for branding, advertising, and digital media.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of design and color theory
  • Working with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator & CorelDRAW
  • Logo design, posters, flyers, and social media graphics
  • Designing for print vs. digital platforms
  • Portfolio development

Duration & Tuition

Duration: 2 Months
Tuition Fee: ₦224,000

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as graphic designers, brand specialists, digital media consultants, and freelancers in creative industries.
