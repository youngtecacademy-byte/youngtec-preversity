Computer Literacy

Master the basics of computer use and digital skills for everyday life and work.

Course Overview

The Computer Literacy program introduces learners to the essentials of using a computer, from understanding hardware and software to practical applications like word processing, internet use, and online safety.

Course Objectives

  • Understand fundamental computer concepts and terminology.
  • Gain proficiency in basic applications such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Develop internet and email communication skills.
  • Apply digital safety practices.

Modules

  • Introduction to Computers & Operating Systems
  • Word Processing Essentials
  • Spreadsheets for Beginners
  • Creating Presentations
  • Internet, Email & Online Safety

Duration

2 Months (Weekend & Weekday classes available)

Tuition

NGN 56,000 (includes course materials and certification)
