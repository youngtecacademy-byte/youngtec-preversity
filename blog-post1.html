New State‑of‑the‑Art Labs Open

Published August 24, 2025 · by Youngtec Team · 4 min read

We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of our state‑of‑the‑art computer and innovation labs—purpose‑built spaces where students learn by doing. From coding and robotics to digital design and AI, these labs are engineered to spark curiosity and accelerate mastery.

Why it matters: Learning tools shape learning outcomes. With pro‑grade equipment and an industry‑style setup, students develop confidence and skills that transfer directly to the real world.

What’s Inside the New Labs

  • High‑Performance Workstations: Latest‑gen multi‑core PCs with SSD storage for blazing‑fast workflows.
  • Dual‑Monitor Setups: Improve focus and productivity during coding, design, and research tasks.
  • Robotics & IoT Bench: Arduino, micro:bit, and sensors for hands‑on electronics and prototyping.
  • Creator Studio: Graphics tablets, photo/video gear, and a mini‑sound booth for content production.
  • Fast & Filtered Network: Secure, high‑speed internet with responsible access controls.
  • Power & Backup: Stabilized power with UPS and generator support for uninterrupted learning.

Designed for Teaching & Teamwork

Each lab features flexible seating, instructor projection, and collaboration corners. Whiteboards and quick‑connect screens make it natural to brainstorm, demo, and iterate. Safety, accessibility, and ergonomics were top priorities throughout the build.

Programs You Can Start Right Away

  • Computer Literacy & Digital Skills
  • Graphics Design & Content Creation
  • Web Design & Full‑Stack Development
  • Mobile App Development
  • Robotics, IoT & Embedded Systems
  • Intro to AI & Data Fundamentals

Visit & Enrollment

Tours are available by appointment. Parents, educators, and prospective students are welcome to experience the labs and speak with our instructors about placements and schedules.
