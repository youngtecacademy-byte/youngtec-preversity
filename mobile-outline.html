Mobile App Development

Build powerful mobile experiences for Android & iOS

Course Overview

This Mobile App Development course equips students with the knowledge and skills to design, build, and deploy high-performance apps for Android and iOS devices. Students will gain practical experience with native and cross-platform tools, preparing them to deliver scalable mobile solutions in todayâ€™s app-driven world.

Course Objectives

  • Understand mobile application architecture and design principles.
  • Develop Android and iOS apps using industry-standard tools.
  • Implement responsive and user-friendly mobile UI/UX designs.
  • Deploy apps to Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
  • Integrate APIs, databases, and cloud services into apps.

Course Outline (Modules)

  1. Introduction to Mobile App Development
  2. Programming Foundations (Java, Kotlin, Swift, Dart)
  3. Android App Development (Studio)
  4. iOS App Development (Xcode & Swift)
  5. Cross-Platform Development (Flutter, React Native)
  6. Mobile UI/UX Design & Prototyping
  7. APIs, Databases & Cloud Integration
  8. App Testing, Debugging & Deployment

Duration

3 Months (Intensive Program)

Who Can Apply

Beginners, aspiring mobile developers, IT professionals, and entrepreneurs interested in creating mobile applications.

What You'll Gain

  • Hands-on mobile app development skills.
  • Portfolio-ready mobile applications.
  • Ability to design, build, and publish apps.
  • Proficiency in cross-platform and native development tools.

Career Opportunities

  • Mobile App Developer (Android/iOS)
  • Cross-Platform Developer (Flutter, React Native)
  • Mobile UI/UX Designer
  • Freelance App Developer
  • Mobile Solutions Engineer
