Video Editing & Motion Graphics

Craft engaging stories with visuals, sound, and motion design

Course Overview

This program provides hands-on training in video production, post-production, and motion design. Students will learn to transform raw footage into compelling visual stories using industry-standard software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve. You will also master color grading, sound mixing, and motion graphics for film, television, and digital media.

Detailed Course Outline

  • Module 1: Fundamentals of Video Editing – Cutting, sequencing, storytelling basics (2 weeks)
  • Module 2: Color Correction & Grading – Tone, mood, and cinematic color workflows (2 weeks)
  • Module 3: Sound Editing & Mixing – Voice-over integration, sound design, and audio balance (2 weeks)
  • Module 4: Motion Graphics & Title Design – Animations, intros, lower-thirds using After Effects (3 weeks)
  • Module 5: Visual Effects & Compositing – Green screen, VFX integration, advanced compositing (3 weeks)
  • Module 6: Project Workflow & Final Delivery – Rendering, formats, client delivery, and teamwork (2 weeks)

Total Duration: 14 weeks (approx. 4 months)
