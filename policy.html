Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters at Youngtec Computers Academy

1. Introduction

At Youngtec Computers Academy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number) when you register.
  • Payment information when you enroll for courses.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our training services.
  • Process payments and course enrollments.
  • Send important updates, offers, and newsletters.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except as required by law. All sensitive data is encrypted and secured using industry best practices.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at:
Email: support@youngtecacademy.com
Phone: +234 907 318 5861

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly to stay informed.
