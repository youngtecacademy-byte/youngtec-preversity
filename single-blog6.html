The Youngtec Computers Scholarship Program is an initiative designed to empower young people in public schools across Nigeria to pursue careers in Information Technology. Through this program, students aged 11 to 20 are given the opportunity to join our academy and receive practical training to prepare for a successful future in tech.

We hold three training orientations each year where students are introduced to technology careers, digital literacy, and coding. Winners of the scholarship will learn **FREE of charge** at Youngtec Computers Academy.
