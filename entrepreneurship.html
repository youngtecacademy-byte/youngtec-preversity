Entrepreneurship with Integrity

Building successful businesses rooted in honesty, ethics, and faith.

Course Overview

This course equips aspiring and current entrepreneurs with the principles and practices needed to start, grow, and sustain businesses that thrive on integrity. By combining ethical leadership, innovative thinking, and sound business models, learners will gain the foundation to create enterprises that generate both profit and impact.

Objectives

  • Understand the role of integrity in sustainable entrepreneurship.
  • Develop entrepreneurial mindsets grounded in faith and innovation.
  • Learn to create business models that deliver value responsibly.
  • Balance profitability with social and ethical impact.

Outline (Modules)

  1. Foundations of Ethical Entrepreneurship
  2. Creativity, Innovation, and Business Planning
  3. Faith and Integrity in Business Operations
  4. Marketing and Customer Relationships
  5. Financial Management for Entrepreneurs
  6. Scaling with Impact and Sustainability

Duration

8 Weeks (Hands-on business projects included)

Who Can Apply

Students, startup founders, business owners, and anyone with the passion to launch or grow an ethical business venture.

What You'll Gain

  • Ability to start and manage a business with integrity.
  • Practical entrepreneurship tools and strategies.
  • Confidence in handling challenges with ethical solutions.
  • A network of like-minded faith-driven entrepreneurs.

Career Opportunities

  • Startup Founder / Entrepreneur
  • Business Development Manager
  • Social Enterprise Leader
  • Faith-based Business Consultant
