Course Overview

The School of Multimedia at Youngtec Computers Academy equips students with cutting-edge skills in graphic design, video production, animation, UI/UX design, and digital content creation. Learn from experienced industry professionals and work on real-world projects that will sharpen your creative and technical abilities.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of design theory and color psychology.
  • Master industry-standard tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud and Figma.
  • Develop video editing, sound design, and animation skills.
  • Create professional portfolios to showcase your work to employers.
  • Collaborate effectively on creative projects.

Curriculum Highlights

Semester 1
  • Introduction to Multimedia
  • Graphic Design Fundamentals
  • Photography Basics
  • Video Production Essentials
Semester 2
  • Advanced Video Editing & Motion Graphics
  • UI/UX Design Principles
  • 3D Modeling & Animation
  • Final Portfolio Project
